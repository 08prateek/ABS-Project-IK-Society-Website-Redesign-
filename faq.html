Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Admission Process??

To begin the admission process, first, submit the application form, ensuring all details are accurate. After submission, the institution will review the form for completeness. Concurrently, gather necessary documents such as transcripts, ID proofs, and photographs for the next steps.

Submit the collected documents to the institution for verification. Once verified, proceed with the payment of the admission fee. Upon successful payment and document verification, receive the final admission confirmation. Attend the orientation program and commence classes as per the academic calendar.

What is the mission of IK Society?

At Islamia Karimia, our mission is to provide an exceptional educational experience that nurtures intellectual curiosity, fosters personal growth, and prepares students to thrive in a rapidly changing world. We are dedicated to offering rigorous academic programs, innovative teaching methods, and supportive learning environments that empower students to achieve their highest potential.

Through our commitment to excellence, inclusivity, and community engagement, we aim to cultivate leaders who are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and ethical values to make a positive impact in their professions and communities.
