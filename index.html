About Our Society

We are one of the largest and most diverse educational societies in India, with over 9000 students and a staff of more than 500 qualified personnel.

Islamia Karimia Society was established in 1902 for the public benefit, and it is recognized globally. Throughout our great history, the Islamia Karimia Society has offered access to a wide range of academic opportunities.
