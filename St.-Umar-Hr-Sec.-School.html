St Umar Higher Secondary School

Home >Institutions >St Umar Higher Secondary School

St. Umar Hr. Sec. School, Indore, is one of the successfully running institutions of Islamia Karimia Society, Indore, established in 1980. It is an English-medium school, recognized by the M.P. Government, running classes from nursery to twelfth with the strength of 4000 students under the guidance of 200 qualified and dedicated staff personnel.

It has an adequate facility for advanced computers and a science lab. Along with various sports activities like fencing, archery floor ball, aerobics, marshal arts, scouting and guide activities, etc., supported by an updated collection of books in the library

At St. Umar Higher Secondary School, we believe in holistic education that goes beyond textbooks and classrooms. Our experienced faculty members are committed to providing quality instruction while instilling values of integrity, compassion, and leadership in our students. With a wide range of academic and extracurricular opportunities, modern facilities, and a vibrant community, we empower our students to excel academically and become lifelong learners. Join us at St. Umar Higher Secondary School and embark on a journey of discovery, growth, and success.
